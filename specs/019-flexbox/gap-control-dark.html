Gap Control — Wrapped 3-Box Layout

A visual gap editor showing 3 flex children in a wrapped arrangement: 2 on the first row, 1 below. Scrubbers sit where the gaps live: gap-x (orange) between the two top boxes, gap-y (teal) on the left between rows, and gap (shorthand) in the center opening. Scrubbers are sized like the Radius / Box Model controls.

  • 2 boxes on row 1, 1 box on row 3 — simulates flex-wrap
  • Between top boxes (↔): orange scrubber for gap-x
  • Left of middle row (↕): teal scrubber for gap-y
  • Center of middle row: gap shorthand (dimmed in split, active in unified)
  • Hover highlights the axis; click opens dropdown; drag scrubs

3 Anatomy & Scrubber Sizing

CSS grid: 3 columns × 3 rows. Boxes at 1/1, 1/3, 3/1. X-scrubber at 1/2. Y-scrubber at 2/1. Gap shorthand at 2/2.

Why this layout: 2 boxes on top + 1 below naturally exposes both gap axes — the horizontal gap is visible between the top two boxes, and the vertical gap is the space before the bottom box. Scrubbers sit exactly where the gap exists, making the spatial relationship intuitive.
